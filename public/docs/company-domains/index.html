# Using a Custom Domain for your Team

In order to get your domain to resolve to the application, you will gave to create a cname in your domain provider's register pointing to $APP_DOMAIN.

For https protocol you may use a cloudflare proxy (opens new window). Alternatively, if you would like to have an ssl certificate installed on our servers, please contact us for a free quote.
